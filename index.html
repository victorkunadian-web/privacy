Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Reliability Pro ("the App") for mobile devices, developed by Kunadian Academy.

1. Information We Collect

Most of the App's engineering tools run entirely on your device and do not require an account. The App also offers an optional account, used to manage a free trial and premium subscription. If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Account identifier (user ID) — an internal identifier generated for your account.
  • Subscription and trial status — timestamps recording your free-trial start and subscription expiry, used to determine your access to premium features.

Sensor features (such as barometer, vibration, and acoustic diagnostics) process readings locally on your device and are not transmitted to or stored by us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above solely to provide account management and app functionality — creating your account, signing you in, and determining your trial and subscription access. We do not use your data for advertising or third-party analytics.

3. Service Providers

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Functions, and Storage) to operate accounts and store the data described above. Firebase processes this data on our behalf under Google's privacy and security terms. Subscription payments are processed by Google Play, Apple, or Stripe, which handle payment information directly; we do not receive or store your full payment card details.

4. Data Security

Data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing kunadian.academy@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request", or via our deletion page. Requests are processed within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed at children.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, contact us at kunadian.academy@gmail.com.
